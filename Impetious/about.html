Welcome to Our School

Impetus Dominion Academy was established in August, 2015, a Co-educational school which operates a Nigerian/British curricula as both seek to achieve different ends. The former based on the National Education Policy, takes learning from a wider perspective while the latter emphasizes the depth of knowledge.
Our school has provision for all the classes from Nursery, Primary, Junior and Senior Secondary.

Our vision is to raise world class leaders whose values are inspired through sound and qualitative education.
We hope to achieve this vision, by creating an enabling environment that will unleash every child's potential and ultimately ensure that stakeholders in education get real value for their investments.
